Welcome to P.C. HARSHAKUMAR & CO.

P.C. HARSHAKUMAR & CO. is a professional financial services and auditing firm primarily based in Kerala, India. The firm is well-established in the Alappuzha region and is recognized for providing a broad spectrum of taxation, auditing, and accounting consultancy services to both individual and corporate clients. Established in 2013 by P.C Harshakumar (MBA Finance), the firm has 13 years of experience in Auditing and Taxation.

The firm is characterized by its localized expertise and its role as a "one-stop shop" for financial compliance in the Alappuzha region. The primary objective of this auditing firm is to examine a company's financial records and express an independent opinion on whether they present a "true and fair view" of the business.

Auditing firms are essential for a healthy investment climate. When an auditing firm "signs off" on a company's books, it gives banks the confidence to lend money and investors the confidence to buy shares. Without this "seal of approval," the flow of capital would slow down due to a lack of trust in financial reporting. This auditing firm acts as a high-stakes financial detective agency, serving as a critical bridge of trust between a company's internal management and its external stakeholders, such as investors, creditors, and regulators.
