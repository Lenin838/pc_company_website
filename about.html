About Our Firm

P.C Harshakumar & Co.

P.C Harshakumar & Co. is a professional auditing and financial consultancy firm established in 2013 by P.C Harshakumar (MBA Finance). The firm provides auditing, taxation, and accounting consultancy services to businesses and individuals across Kerala. With 13 years of experience in the field, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the Alappuzha region.

We act as a "trust partner" for our clients, ensuring financial transparency, compliance, and strategic growth through ethical and high-quality financial services.

Vision

The vision of P.C HARSHAKUMAR & CO is to be a leading force in promoting financial inclusion, economic empowerment, and social development in the communities it serves. By upholding auditing principles and values, the firm strives to become a trusted partner and catalyst for positive change, fostering sustainable growth and prosperity for its members and the broader society. Through innovative services, responsible governance, and active community engagement, the firm aims to be a beacon of financial resilience, ensuring equitable access to financial services and opportunities for all.

Mission

The mission of P.C HARSHAKUMAR & CO. is centred on delivering high-quality, ethical, and client-centric financial services that empower businesses to thrive in a complex regulatory environment. We are committed to:

  • Ensure Total Compliance: Protect clients from legal and financial risks by ensuring rigorous adherence to GST, Income Tax, and auditing standards.
  • Simplify Financial Complexity: Demystify intricate tax laws (like Cess and ITR filing) for small and medium enterprises (SMEs), making professional financial management accessible to all business sizes.
  • Foster Strategic Growth: Beyond "doing the math," provide strategic advice that helps clients minimize tax liabilities and maximize operational efficiency.
  • Uphold Professional Integrity: As a firm of Chartered Accountants, we are committed to ICAI standards of independence and transparency, acting as a "watchdog" for the public interest.

Through these efforts, we strive to contribute to the holistic development and prosperity of our members and the communities we serve.

Core Objectives

The core objective of P.C HARSHAKUMAR & CO. is to provide reliable, tech-enabled financial oversight that ensures local businesses remain compliant while optimizing their tax efficiency.

Precision in Compliance

To minimize legal risks for clients by ensuring 100% accuracy in GST, TDS, and Income Tax filings.

Operational Efficiency

To streamline the financial back-office of businesses through professional "Accounting Outsourcing Services," allowing owners to focus on core operations.

Tax Optimization

To provide expert consultancy on Cess and Income Tax, helping clients leverage legal provisions to reduce their tax burden effectively.

Data Integrity

To maintain the highest standards of auditing that provide an authentic "true and fair" view of a company's financial health to stakeholders and banks.

Tax Consultancy

To provide tax consultancy services including preparation and filing of income tax returns.

GST Compliance

To offer GST (Goods and Services Tax) compliance and filing assistance to clients.
